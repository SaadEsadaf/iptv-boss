## 3. PRICING STRATEGY

### Current Pricing
| Plan | Duration | Price | Monthly Equivalent | Savings |
|------|----------|-------|-------------------|---------|
| Trial | 24 hours | FREE | - | - |
| Monthly | 1 month | €12.99 | €12.99 | - |
| Quarterly | 3 months | €29.99 | €10.00 | 23% |
| Yearly | 12 months | €99.00 | €8.25 | 37% |

### Pricing Psychology
- **Decoy effect:** Monthly €12.99 vs Yearly €99 — makes yearly look cheap
- **Anchor pricing:** "Was €19.99, now €12.99" — 35% off perception
- **Bundle pricing:** "IPTV + VPN = €15.99" — value perception
- **Trial first:** FREE 24h — reduces risk, increases trust
- **Urgency pricing:** "24 hours only — €9.99" — flash sales
- **Subscription model:** Recurring revenue > one-time

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Price point: €9.99 vs €12.99 vs €14.99
- Plan names: "Basic" vs "Premium" vs "Elite"
- Trial duration: 24h vs 24h vs 7 days
- Payment options: PayPal vs Stripe vs both
- Checkout flow: 1-page vs 3-step
- Upsell timing: Immediate vs 24h later
